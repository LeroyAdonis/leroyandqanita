Reception
  • Accolades Boutique Venue 72 Dale Road, President Park Midrand - Ivory Hall
  • 18 Dec 2021, 4PM - 12AM

The Ivory Hall is a intimate and private wedding reception hall that comfortably seats 120 guests with a dance floor. Open trusses, large concertina windows and doors provides for an outdoor feeling while still under roof. It is flanked by the ever popular and magnificent Chestnut tree, outside ceremonial area. This popular self contained wedding venue features an entrance area that conveniently interconnects with its buffet room and inside chapel while opening onto a picturesque entertainment area with pool, bar, water features and beautifully manicured gardens.
